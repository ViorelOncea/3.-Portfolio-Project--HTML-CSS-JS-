About Me

Hi! I'm Viorel, a musical composer and conductor (graduated the Conservatory) that discovered that he really likes coding. I like everything about it: the structure, how it works, the creativity associated with programming and many other aspects that surround this particular field.

I also like sports. I play football and tennis (actually these 2 sports are the ones I also follow 😀). My favourite team is F.C. Barcelona and my favourite player is Lionel Messi (he's a genius). As far as tennis goes, I like the "BIG THREE" (Roger Federer, Novak Djokovic and Rafael Nadal) and a really good and exciting young player named Carlos Alcaraz (also spanish , just like Nadal).

If free time still remains, I read or listen to discussions about ideas and anything else that seem interesting.

So here I am, a musician that is passionate about sports, enjoying learning how to code.
